we're changing the way people think about banking

standard bank is a financial technology company founded on the premise that basic banking services should be helpful. easy and free. we want to profit with our members, not from them. that's why our model doesn't rely on overdraft. monthly service, minimum balance, or other consumer fees. we partner with regional banks to design member first financial products. this creates a more competitive market with better, lower-cost options for everyday americans who are'n being served well by traditional bank. we help drive innovation, inclusion, and access acrose the industry.

our mission

standard bank exists becauce we belive everyone deserves financial peace of minde. we'r building a new kind of online bank account that helps members get ahead by making managing money easy. it's your money. it's your life.

our culture.

we belives that creating a company where people love to work is built on relationship. growth, and impact, we cultivate a culture of low ego and set a high bar for excellence. we believe in flat organizationa and open work spaces. we treat one another inside the company the same way we treat our members-by communicating in ways that buld trust, operating with transparency, and having one another's back. we recognize the contributions of individuals and celebrate wins as a team. every member of the team is an owner of the company and is encouraged to take on any part of the business. what we all have in common is a mission that starts with heart, and a desire to work together to create somthing of lasting value and personal impact for our employees and members alike.
